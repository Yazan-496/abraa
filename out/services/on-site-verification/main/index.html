On site Verification

Any supplier can sign up for a free Abraa.com account and start displaying its products. However, without having to go through a verification procedure, there's no method to know if this supplier is genuine or fraudulent. A Gold Supplier or Platinum Supplier, on the other hand, should be verified by a 3rd-party inspector. This inspector rigorously examines the supplier's business license and contact details to verify that the Gold Supplier or Platinum Supplier is a genuine company. After it's been authenticated and verified, a Gold Supplier or Platinum Supplier will receive a Verified Badg. A Platinum Supplier or Gold Supplier in UAE must go through a one more step. A Dubailist.com staff must visit their offices as well as facilities to further verify on-site check. The verification of suppliers on Abraa.com serves to safeguard buyers by making sure that suppliers are legally registered companies. Verifications of the companies are carried out by both Dubailist.com and/or independent verification companies. Dubailist provides on-site verification service to both Gold and Platinum Suppliers. To offer an exceptional service, we have a team of experienced professionals who severely checked supplier's company's premises and make sure onsite operations exist there. Also, the supplierslegal status and other related details and information are then verified by a 3rd-party verification agency.
